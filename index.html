Bio and Education: Meet Matt

Registered Physiotherapist

MScPT, MSc Neuroscience, BSc (Hons)

Matt began his academic journey at Carleton University, graduating in 2015 with a Bachelor of Science degree in Neuroscience and Mental Health. He then completed his Master of Science degree within the field of Neuroscience at Queen’s University in 2017, exploring how eye movements can be used as a potential biomarker to diagnose and assess neurological disorders, such as Alzheimer’s disease. He was awarded the prestigious Frederick Banting and Charles Best Canada Graduate Scholarship for his research and accomplishments.

After completing his thesis, Matt pursued a second Master of Science degree in Physical Therapy at Queen’s University, graduating in 2019. Matt has furthered his clinical training by completing courses in soft tissue release, advanced manual therapy, dry needling/acupuncture, pain neuroscience, and vestibular rehabilitation techniques.

Matt has a special interest in treating a wide variety of clients, ranging from elite athletes to clients suffering from debilitating chronic pain. He also has experience treating clients suffering from vertigo, cervicogenic headaches, running injuries, and lower back pain, both acute and chronic.

Matt is passionate about accelerating his clients’ recovery from injury. He values compassion, always striving to understand his client’s values and individualizes their rehabilitation to suit their functional needs and goals. He strives to implement evidence-informed techniques into his practice, remaining up to date on effective treatment strategies and educational approaches. When not at work, Matt loves to be outdoors camping, fishing, golfing and lifting weights.

Services

Injury Rehabilitation and Prevention

As adaptive as the human body is, it is nearly impossible to go through our busy lives without sustaining an injury. Unfortunately, both acute and chronic pain can prevent you from participating in activities that are meaningful to you (i.e., walking, sports, work). I am here to work with you as a team to empower you and achieve your goals, whether they be to excel as an athlete or to hold your grandchildren without pain. I will assess the musculoskeletal and neurological systems during my assessment, and together, we will create an individualized treatment plan that works best for you to help you manage and prevent injury recurrence.

Surgical Recovery

If you have recently had a surgery (i.e., rotator cuff, hip or knee replacement, etc.) or are preparing to have one, I will work with you to create a safe, effective, and evidence-based rehabilitation program. Together we will work on range of motion and strengthening exercises to accelerate your recovery.

Dizziness and Vertigo Treatment

When it feels like the world is spinning, it can be quite challenging to complete even the simplest daily tasks. I will conduct a thorough neurological and vestibular assessment to find the source of your dizziness, and create an effective treatment program that works for you.

Chronic Headache Management

Do you suffer from chronic tension headaches or migraines? The source of most headaches can occur from the muscles, joints and/or nerves within the neck, jaw and shoulder(s). I will do a thorough examination to evaluate the cause of your headaches and will develop an effective exercise routine to reduce the frequency and alleviate your headaches.

Delivery of Services

In-Person

Clients within Orillia, Barrie, and Simcoe regions can be assessed and treated in-person, within the clinic. Before coming into the clinic, we will gather your contact information and provide a COVID-19 pre-screen to maintain appropriate infection control standards and procedures.

Tele-Health

Initial assessments and follow-up treatment appointments are delivered virtually using a video conferencing platform that complies with the privacy requirements set out by the College of Physiotherapists of Ontario.

Direct Billing

We electronically submit claims and directly bill to most insurance companies to offer convenience for clients. Please contact your insurance company to access information regarding your coverage.

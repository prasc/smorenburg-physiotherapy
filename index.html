Services

Injury Rehabilitation and Pain Management

Have an injury, discomfort or pain that is preventing you from engaging in activities that you find enjoyable (e.g. walking, running, sports, gardening)? We are here to get you back to just that! With a thorough assessment, we determine the root cause of your concern and its contributing factors in order to develop a comprehensive treatment plan that will help to prevent and manage future injury.

Concussion Management

Concussions are quite common in contact sports but often go unrecognized and untreated. Here at Kumi Physio we take a patient-centered approach where we provide education and recommendations to the patient and their family about managing concussions. This includes rehabilitation exercises, energy management strategies, psychosocial considerations, and gradual Return to School/Work/Play.

Sport Performance Optimization

Are you an individual who strives to maximize your sport performance? At Kumi Physio we look at how you move and consider the demands and needs of your sport(s) in order to provide sport-specific physiotherapy and training. Sport-specific strengthening and power exercises, mobility and agility drills, and cross-training will be incorporated to optimize performance.

Delivery of Services

Telerehabilitation

Assessments and treatments are delivered virtually using a secure video conferencing platform. This convenient method reduces the need to travel and allows for flexible booking times.

In-Home

Clients within the Guelph region are able to have our physiotherapist assess and treat from the convenience of their home. All necessary equipment will be brought to your appointment.

In-Clinic

Physiotherapy assessments and treatments provided in person at our clinic.

Direct Billing

Kumi Physio offers convenient invoicing for our clients. We are able to electronically submit claims to most insurance companies. Please contact your insurance company directly to access information about your policy and coverage.

Meet Frank

Frank Kumi, MScPT, B.Kin

Registered Physiotherapist

Frank Kumi grew up in Toronto playing competitive soccer and basketball. Frank was drawn into the rehabilitation community after going through several injuries himself. After completing his Bachelor of Science Degree in Kinesiology from York University, Frank obtained a Master of Science Degree in Physical Therapy from Queen’s University. Frank has furthered his clinical education by completing courses on Post-Concussion Management, Level 1 Manual Therapy, Soft Tissue Release, Acupuncture, and Pain Neuroscience education.

Frank encourages an active approach to treatment by providing his clients with the education and exercises necessary to take control of their injury, as well as supplementing it with manual therapy as needed. As a lover of sports, you can find Frank playing basketball, soccer, volleyball, or working out when not at the clinic. Frank looks forward to building a strong therapeutic alliance with his clients and empowering them to achieve their goals!
